What is Interactive Voice Response (IVR)?

Interactive Voice Response (IVR) is an automated phone system that helps businesses interact with customers using voice menus and prerecorded messages. Users can respond by pressing phone keys or speaking, enabling self-service or directing them to the appropriate department or agent. IVR enhances efficiency, minimizes wait times, and delivers a more convenient and personalized customer experience.

VOIP Services

VoIP texting allows users to send and receive SMS messages using a phone number provided by a VoIP service. This number can be a landline, toll-free, or a virtual number used for business communication. It enables businesses to integrate texting with their existing VoIP phone system, streamlining communication. VoIP texting is useful for customer support, marketing, and internal messaging without needing a separate mobile line. It enhances flexibility and accessibility, allowing messages to be managed from multiple devices.

How does VOIP work?

1. Voice to Digital Conversion

When you speak into a VoIP-enabled device (VoIP phone, computer, or mobile app), your voice is converted into digital packets using a codec.

2. Packet Transmission

These digital voice packets are then compressed and transmitted over the internet using the Internet Protocol (IP).

3. Cost-Effective

Using bulk voice calls is cost-effective for reaching a wide audience without high expenses.

4. High Response Rates

Phone calls are more attention-grabbing than text messages or emails, so your message has a better chance of being heard.

5. Easy to Use

Using user-friendly software, you can easily record messages, upload contact lists, and schedule calls without needing technical skills.
